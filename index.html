About Us

Nurturing Future
Generations

At Taska Sherry, we have dedicated ourselves to creating a space where children don't just learn—they flourish. Founded in 2010, our center has grown from a small neighborhood playgroup into one of Johor Bahru's leading early childhood education centers, conveniently located in Mount Austin.

Our philosophy blends modern educational science with the warmth of a loving home. We believe that every child deserves a start in life that is secure, stimulating, and full of joy.

A Predictable Day

Routine creates safety. Here is how your child spends their day.

08:00 AM

Welcome & Health Check

Gentle transition from parents, temperature check, and hand washing.

10:00 AM

Guided Learning & Exploration

Montessori-inspired activities focusing on fine motor skills and focus.

12:30 PM

Nutritious Lunch & Hygiene

Organic balanced meals followed by teeth brushing and face washing.

02:00 PM

Rest & Quiet Time

Low lighting, soft music, and designated nap areas for restoration.

04:30 PM

Pickup & Reflection

Calm departure with daily report handover to parents.
